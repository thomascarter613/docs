# Client Delivery Workflows

Client Delivery Workflows provide repeatable structures for implementation work.

Use these workflows as the baseline for engagements.

---

## Kickoff workflow

1. Confirm goals
2. Confirm scope
3. Confirm stakeholders
4. Confirm timeline
5. Confirm communication channel
6. Confirm access needs
7. Confirm risks
8. Confirm next step

---

## Workflow mapping workflow

1. Identify the process
2. Identify trigger events
3. Identify people involved
4. Identify systems involved
5. Identify handoffs
6. Identify failure points
7. Identify automation opportunities
8. Document current state

---

## Implementation workflow

1. Define improved workflow
2. Confirm assumptions
3. Build first version
4. Test with sample data or scenario
5. Review with client
6. Adjust
7. Document
8. Prepare handoff

---

## Handoff workflow

1. Explain what was delivered
2. Show how it works
3. Document how to use it
4. Identify known limitations
5. Explain support path
6. Confirm acceptance
7. Capture follow-up opportunities

---

## Post-engagement workflow

1. Review what worked
2. Review what failed
3. Extract reusable assets
4. Extract product requirements
5. Update delivery templates
6. Add lessons to Founder Log
