# Daily Log Template

Use this page as the canonical template for daily Founder Log entries.

A daily log captures what happened, why it mattered, what changed, and what should be reviewed later. It does not need to be polished. It does need to be specific enough that the future founder, a collaborator, or the AI Cofounder can recover the operating context.

* What changed?
* What mattered most?
* What should not be forgotten?
