# Discovery Questions

These questions are used to understand a prospect’s business, workflows, pain, readiness, and fit.

Use them as a guide, not a rigid script.

---

## Business context

- What does your business do?
- Who do you serve?
- How large is the team?
- What roles are involved in daily operations?
- What systems or tools do you currently depend on?

---

## Pain and friction

- What work feels repetitive, manual, or error-prone?
- Where do tasks get delayed?
- What information gets lost?
- What work depends too much on one person remembering it?
- What do you wish happened automatically?

---

## Workflow mapping

- Walk me through the process from start to finish.
- What triggers this workflow?
- Who touches it?
- What tools are involved?
- Where does information move from one system to another?
- Where do mistakes usually happen?

---

## Cost of the problem

- How much time does this take each week?
- What happens when this process fails?
- Does this affect revenue, customer experience, compliance, or staff workload?
- What would improve if this workflow became easier?

---

## Current alternatives

- What have you tried already?
- What tools are you using now?
- Why has the current approach not solved the problem?
- Are there tools you tried and abandoned?

---

## AI readiness

- Have you used AI tools in the business yet?
- What concerns do you have about AI?
- What would make AI feel useful rather than risky?
- Are there tasks you would not want automated?

---

## Buying process

- Who would be involved in approving this?
- What budget range would make sense if the value is clear?
- Is this urgent, important, or exploratory?
- What would need to be true for you to move forward?

---

## Success criteria

- What would a successful implementation look like?
- What measurable improvement would matter?
- What would make this feel worth the investment?
- What would make this project fail?

---

## Closing question

- Based on what we discussed, would it be useful for me to outline a possible implementation path?
