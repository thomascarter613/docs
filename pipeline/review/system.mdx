# Pipeline Review System

The Pipeline Review System turns sales activity into business intelligence.

It helps Applied Innovation Corp understand what is working, what is confusing buyers, where deals stall, and what needs to change.

---

## Purpose

Pipeline review exists to answer:

- How many prospects entered the pipeline?
- Where did they come from?
- What problems did they mention?
- What objections appeared?
- Which offers were understood?
- Where did deals stall?
- What should change next?

---

## Review cadence

Review pipeline weekly.

Monthly reviews should identify larger patterns across pipeline activity.

---

## Review inputs

Use:

- discovery notes
- sales notes
- pricing feedback
- prospect objections
- follow-up status
- closed-won and closed-lost notes
- published content performance
- referrals and inbound signals

---

## Review outputs

A completed pipeline review should produce:

- next follow-up actions
- updated objections list
- improved discovery questions
- pricing notes
- offer changes
- positioning changes
- narrative ideas
- risks or assumptions

---

## Pipeline stages

Recommended stages:

1. Lead
2. Contacted
3. Discovery scheduled
4. Discovery completed
5. Qualified
6. Proposal sent
7. Negotiation or review
8. Closed won
9. Closed lost
10. Nurture

---

## Definition of done

Pipeline review is working when sales activity consistently improves positioning, offer clarity, pricing confidence, and follow-up discipline.
