# Sales System

The Sales System converts qualified discovery into clear offers and buying decisions.

Sales is not persuasion detached from reality. Sales is the process of helping the right buyer understand the value of solving a real problem.

---

## Purpose

The Sales System exists to:

- clarify the problem
- frame the cost of inaction
- define the desired outcome
- explain the implementation path
- reduce uncertainty
- present pricing clearly
- create a confident next step

---

## Sales principles

Applied Innovation Corp sales should be:

- consultative
- specific
- outcome-led
- honest about fit
- clear about scope
- grounded in discovery
- free of AI hype
- focused on business value

---

## Sales flow

1. Confirm the problem
2. Restate the current workflow
3. Name the business impact
4. Explain the opportunity
5. Present the implementation path
6. Address risks and constraints
7. Present pricing
8. Ask for a clear decision or next step

---

## Good-fit sales

A good-fit sales opportunity has:

- clear pain
- clear decision maker
- realistic budget
- business impact
- implementation feasibility
- willingness to change workflow
- trust in the process

---

## Definition of done

The Sales System is working when qualified prospects understand the offer, the value, the price, the implementation path, and the next step.
