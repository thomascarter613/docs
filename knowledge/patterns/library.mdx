# Pattern Library

The Pattern Library records recurring patterns discovered through founder work, delivery, sales, platform development, and governance.

---

## Initial patterns

| Pattern | Area | Meaning | Response |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| AI needs context | AI Cofounder | AI assistance improves when context is structured | Build handoff and memory systems |
| Automation requires workflow clarity | Delivery | Automating unclear processes creates risk | Map workflows before implementation |
| Buyer language matters | Pipeline | Prospects care about outcomes more than AI novelty | Lead with operational relief |
| Founder memory must become infrastructure | Governance | Important context is otherwise lost | Maintain Founder Log and reviews |
| Consulting should produce product intelligence | Platform | Delivery work can reveal reusable requirements | Use asset extraction after engagements |
| Decisions need lineage | Architecture | Future work depends on knowing why choices were made | Maintain ADRs and decision records |
