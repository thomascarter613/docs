# Lineage Map

The Lineage Map shows how major systems and artifacts connect back to their source.

---

## Initial lineage map

| Source | Produces | Destination | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Founder Logs | Decisions, assumptions, risks, context | Governance / ADRs / Knowledge Graph | Preserve operating memory |
| Weekly Reviews | Patterns and priorities | Monthly Themes / Pipeline / Delivery | Convert activity into intelligence |
| Monthly Themes | Doctrine and strategy | Narrative / Governance / Platform | Convert patterns into direction |
| Discovery Calls | Buyer pain and objections | Pipeline / Narrative / Offers | Improve sales and positioning |
| Client Delivery | Templates and requirements | Delivery / Platform | Convert work into reusable assets |
| ADRs | Architecture decisions | Platform / Knowledge Graph | Preserve technical decision lineage |
| Asset Extraction | SOPs, templates, product requirements | Delivery / Platform / Governance | Reuse operational learning |

---

## Map review questions

- Are important artifacts traceable?
- Are there orphaned docs with no source?
- Are decisions linked to evidence?
- Are platform modules linked to real problems?
- Are public claims linked to proof?
- Are reusable assets linked to delivery or founder work?

---

## Rule of thumb

If an artifact matters, its origin should be visible.
