# Lineage Overview

Lineage traces ideas, decisions, assets, modules, and knowledge back to their source.

It answers the question:

> Where did this come from, and why does it exist?

---

## Purpose

Lineage exists to:

- preserve origin context
- support trust
- prevent orphaned ideas
- connect decisions to evidence
- connect assets to real work
- support AI-assisted reasoning
- make change safer

---

## What needs lineage

Track lineage for:

- ADRs
- platform modules
- governance rules
- reusable assets
- major templates
- derived concepts
- patterns
- public claims
- product requirements

---

## Lineage fields

| Field | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Source | Where the item came from |
| Reason | Why it exists |
| Evidence | What supports it |
| Destination | Where it lives now |
| Related decisions | ADRs or governance records |
| Status | Current lifecycle state |

---

## Definition of done

Lineage is working when important artifacts can be traced back to their origin without relying on memory.
