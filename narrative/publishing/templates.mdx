# Publishing Templates

Publishing Templates provide repeatable structures for turning founder insight into public content.

Use these templates to publish faster without losing clarity or substance.

---

## Founder observation

```md
Today I noticed:

[Observation]

Why it matters:

[Business meaning]

What it changes:

[Implication]

The lesson:

[Clear takeaway]
```

## Build Update

This week I worked on:

[Work completed]

The problem it solves:

[Problem]

Why it matters:

[Importance]

What comes next:

[Next step]
```

## Market Lesson

A pattern I keep seeing:

[Pattern]

The common mistake:

[Mistake]

The better approach:

[Approach]

What this means for businesses:

[Business implication]
```

## Founder Thesis Post

I believe:

[Thesis]

Because:

[Reason]

Most teams miss this because:

[Misunderstanding]

The practical implication:

[Actionable takeaway]
```

## Case-study style lesson

Situation:

[Generalized client or workflow context]

Problem:

[Problem]

Approach:

[Approach]

Lesson:

[Reusable lesson]

Takeaway:

[What others can apply]
```

## Redaction reminder

Before publishing, remove:

* client names
* private numbers
* confidential workflow details
* unsupported claims
* private founder notes
* anything not safe for public reuse
