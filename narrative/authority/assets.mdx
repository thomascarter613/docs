# Authority Assets

Authority Assets are reusable public or semi-public materials that demonstrate Applied Innovation Corp’s thinking, judgment, and capability.

They help buyers understand the company before a sales conversation.

---

| Asset | Audience | Status | Source | Use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

| Monad-Workspace Architecture Map | Developers / technical buyers | Proposed | Platform Engine | Technical authority |

---

## Asset creation standard

Each authority asset should define:

- audience
- problem
- insight
- practical use
- proof source
- call to action
- reuse path

---

## Asset format options

Authority assets may become:

- landing pages
- PDFs
- posts
- essays
- diagrams
- checklists
- templates
- public docs
- sales enablement pages
- onboarding material

---

## Review checklist

Before publishing an authority asset, confirm:

- Is it useful?
- Is it true?
- Is it specific?
- Is it safe to share?
- Does it support positioning?
- Does it show judgment?
- Does it invite the right next step?

---

## Rule of thumb

An authority asset should make the company more trusted even before the buyer speaks to the founder.
