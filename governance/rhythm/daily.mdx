# Daily Operating Rhythm

The Daily Operating Rhythm defines how the founder captures context, executes work, and preserves continuity.

Daily rhythm should be lightweight enough to maintain.

---

## Start-of-day questions

Ask:

- What matters most today?
- What is already in motion?
- What decision is needed?
- What risk should be watched?
- What should the AI Cofounder know before helping?

---

## During the day

Capture:

- decisions
- assumptions
- blockers
- useful observations
- sales signals
- delivery lessons
- platform ideas
- follow-up actions

---

## End-of-day questions

Ask:

- What happened?
- What changed?
- What did I learn?
- What needs follow-up?
- What should be reviewed weekly?
- What should become reusable?

---

## Definition of done

Daily rhythm is working when the founder can resume work tomorrow without losing the thread.
