# Weekly Operating Rhythm

The Weekly Operating Rhythm defines the review and planning cadence for Applied Innovation Corp and Monad-Workspace.

Weekly rhythm turns daily activity into intentional execution.

---

## Weekly review inputs

Use:

- Daily Logs
- pipeline notes
- delivery notes
- platform progress
- governance decisions
- open assumptions
- active risks
- AI Cofounder outputs

---

## Weekly review outputs

Produce:

- weekly summary
- confirmed decisions
- open assumptions
- updated risks
- next priorities
- promotion candidates
- AI Cofounder handoff

---

## Weekly questions

Ask:

- What happened this week?
- What changed?
- What repeated?
- What matters next?
- What needs review?
- What should be promoted?
- What should be stopped?
- What should be delegated to AI?

---

## Weekly planning

After review, define:

- top priorities
- key deliverables
- follow-up actions
- risks to watch
- decisions needed
- publishing opportunities

---

## Definition of done

Weekly rhythm is working when each week begins with clearer priorities than the previous week.
