# Documentation maintenance checklist

Use this checklist during monthly documentation maintenance.

---

## Page health

Check:

- titles
- descriptions
- statuses
- updated dates
- review cycles
- parent links
- child links
- stale pages
- duplicate pages
- broken links

---

## Navigation health

Check:

- sidebar clutter
- nested group clarity
- overview pages
- root pages
- directory listings
- missing pages
- pages listed in `docs.json` that do not exist
- important pages missing from `docs.json`

---

## Search health

Check:

- common search terms
- poor search results
- missing canonical terms
- pages needing better titles
- pages needing better descriptions
- overboosted pages
- underboosted canonical pages

---

## Governance health

Check:

- accepted ADRs
- superseded ADRs
- governance rules
- decision filters
- documentation standards
- human verification rules
- AI promotion rules

---

## Safety health

Check for:

- secrets
- credentials
- private client data
- private personal information
- unsupported public claims
- unreviewed AI-generated doctrine
- unapproved pricing or scope commitments

---

## Changelog

Update `changelog.mdx` for meaningful changes.

Record:

- added pages
- major edits
- navigation changes
- status changes
- supersessions
- governance updates

---

## Definition of done

Monthly maintenance is complete when the docs are accurate, safe, navigable, searchable, and aligned with current operating doctrine.
